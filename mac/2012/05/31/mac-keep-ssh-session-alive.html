Mac: Keep ssh session alive

31 May 2012

When using Terminal and ssh to connect to your server the session will die after some time because of inactivity.

To keep the session alive add this to yourÂ /etc/ssh_config:

ServerAliveInterval 300
ServerAliveCountMax 3

I recommend iTerm as an alternative to Terminal under OS X.
